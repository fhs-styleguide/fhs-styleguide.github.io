Brand Guide

Introduction

As Dabilu Sports is currently in the process of transforming from a small StartUp to a bigger corporation we felt the need to develop a Styleguide for our brand. It consists of three main parts: the Brand Guide, the defined CSS Methologies and the used Component Libraries.

The aims of this Styleguide are:

  • to provide our developers, designers and copywriters with a consistent structure and orientation when creating new components for our brand-website
  • to ensure that the public has the best possible experience when interacting with our website

Colors

The Primary Brand Colors are used to represent Dabidu Sports in public and must reflect the Dabidu Sports brand. These colors are part of our corporate identity.

Typography

Headings

A default base size of 16px is assumed. A scale of 1.333 (Perfect Fourth) is used for increased clarity. Open Sans is to be used throughout, as it TODO

Heading level 1

3.157em (50.15px) - Extra large

<h1 class="heading heading--extra-large">...</h1>

Heading level 2

2.369em (37.904px) - Large

<h2 class="heading heading--large">...</h2>

Heading level 3

1.777em (28.432px) - Medium

<h3 class="heading heading--medium">...</h3>

Heading level 4

1.333em (21.328px) - Small

<h4 class="heading heading--small">...</h4>

To keep a clear and simple structure, heading level 5 (h5) and 6 (h6) must not be used.
